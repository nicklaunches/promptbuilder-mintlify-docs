## Support

Email us at `support@promptbuilder.cc` and include:

- A short summary of the issue
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if helpful)

## Billing

For billing questions, include your account email and any invoice IDs.

## Security

If you believe you found a security issue, email `support@promptbuilder.cc` with "Security" in the subject line.
