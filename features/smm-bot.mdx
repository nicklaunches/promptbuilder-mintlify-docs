SMM Bot is a two-pane workspace for generating social content: fill in a quick brief, generate platform-ready copy, then refine it in chat.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Write a content brief">
    Provide:
    - Topic
    - Tone (or custom tone)
    - Target audience (or custom audience)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a platform">
    Supported platforms include:
    - X
    - LinkedIn
    - Instagram
    - TikTok
    - Reddit
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tune platform options">
    Options change by platform (examples):
    - X: thread length, hashtags, CTA
    - LinkedIn: post type, industry focus, length, emoji
    - Instagram: content type, caption length, hashtag count, CTA
    - TikTok: video length, style, hook style, suggest sounds
    - Reddit: subreddit (optional), post type, title style, TL;DR
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate, then refine in chat">
    Click **Generate** to create the first version, then iterate with follow-ups like:
    - “Give 3 variations”
    - “Make it more punchy / more professional”
    - “Add a stronger hook and CTA”
    - “Rewrite as a LinkedIn carousel / Instagram reel script”
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Save and reuse

When you land on a version you like, save it to **My Prompts** so you can reuse the format later.

## Tips

- Specify your goal: awareness vs conversion vs engagement.
- Give constraints: word count, CTA style, banned phrases, brand voice.
- Ask for multiple variants, then refine the best one.
