## Common issues

**Assistant responses are empty**
- Refresh the page and try again.
- Switch to another assistant model and retry.

**Generate or Optimize button is disabled**
- Make sure the input field has content.
- Check for required fields (model selection, topic).

**Prompts are not saving**
- Confirm you are signed in.
- Refresh and retry the Save action.

## Still stuck?

Email `support@promptbuilder.cc` with steps to reproduce and screenshots.
