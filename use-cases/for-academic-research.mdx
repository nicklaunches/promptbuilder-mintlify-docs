Prompt Builder helps researchers create reliable prompts for analysis, summarization, and writing support.

## Common goals

- Summarize papers consistently
- Build structured outlines for writing
- Compare findings across sources

## A simple workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Draft a research prompt">
    Use **Prompt Generator** to build a structured prompt for your task.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Iterate in Assistant">
    Refine the prompt to match your format and citation needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and reuse">
    Store the final prompt in **My Prompts** for future studies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example research workflows

- Literature review summaries
- Outline generation for papers
- Hypothesis framing
- Data interpretation notes
- Interview or survey question drafts
