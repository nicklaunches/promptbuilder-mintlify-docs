Prompt Builder helps agencies create reusable prompt systems across clients and deliverables.

## Common goals

- Keep client outputs consistent
- Speed up campaign and content production
- Reuse proven prompt templates across accounts

## A simple workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create client templates">
    Use **Prompt Generator** to draft prompts for repeatable deliverables.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optimize for quality">
    Run prompts through **Prompt Optimizer** to standardize output format.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Organize in the Library">
    Save prompts in **My Prompts** and pin client favorites.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example agency workflows

- Client onboarding briefs
- Content calendar templates
- Ad creative variations
- Monthly performance summaries
- Brand voice guidelines
