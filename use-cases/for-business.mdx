## Common goals

- Maintain consistency in tone and format

## A simple workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a baseline prompt">
    Use **Prompt Generator** to draft a structured prompt for a repeatable task.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optimize for clarity">
    Run it through **Prompt Optimizer** to tighten wording and add constraints.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Standardize in the Library">
    Save the prompt to **My Prompts** and pin it for team reuse.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example business workflows

- Customer support replies
- Policy explanations and internal SOPs
- Sales call follow-ups
- Meeting recaps and action items
- Vendor evaluation summaries
