Prompt Builder helps you turn ideas into reliable content workflows with reusable prompts.

## Common goals

- Create consistent outlines and drafts
- Keep tone and structure aligned across pieces
- Build repeatable prompts for different content types

## A simple workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate a draft prompt">
    Use **Prompt Generator** to create a structured prompt for the content type.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Refine the output">
    Use **Prompt Assistant** to iterate on tone, length, and format.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the template">
    Store the prompt in **My Prompts** and reuse it across future content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example content workflows

- Blog post outlines
- Video scripts
- Newsletter drafts
- Social post templates
- Product update summaries
