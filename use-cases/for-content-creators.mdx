Prompt Builder helps creators build repeatable prompts for content production across channels.

## Common goals

- Produce content faster without losing quality
- Reuse formats that already perform well
- Keep tone consistent across platforms

## A simple workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate a content prompt">
    Use **Prompt Generator** to create a structured prompt for your content type.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Iterate in Assistant">
    Use **Prompt Assistant** to refine tone, length, and style.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save winning formats">
    Store prompts in **My Prompts** and reuse them for new content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example creator workflows

- YouTube scripts and hooks
- TikTok concepts and captions
- Newsletter outlines
- Podcast show notes
- Repurposing long-form content
