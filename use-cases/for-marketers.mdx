Prompt Builder helps marketers create consistent prompts for campaigns, messaging, and growth experiments.

## Common goals

- Generate ad copy variations quickly
- Keep brand voice consistent across channels
- Standardize campaign briefs and positioning

## A simple workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Draft a campaign prompt">
    Use **Prompt Generator** to turn a goal into a structured prompt.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optimize the prompt">
    Run it through **Prompt Optimizer** to improve clarity and output format.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save for reuse">
    Store the final prompt in **My Prompts** and pin it for future campaigns.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example marketing workflows

- Landing page copy drafts
- Ad creative variants
- Email campaign sequences
- SEO outlines and meta descriptions
- Positioning and messaging tests
