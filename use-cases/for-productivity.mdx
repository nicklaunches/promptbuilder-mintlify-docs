Prompt Builder helps you build prompts that support planning, prioritization, and daily execution.

## Common goals

- Turn ideas into clear action plans
- Standardize weekly reviews
- Make decisions with consistent criteria

## A simple workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Draft a personal prompt">
    Use **Prompt Generator** to create a structured prompt for a routine.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Refine the output format">
    Use **Prompt Assistant** to tune length, tone, and structure.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save as a template">
    Store the prompt in **My Prompts** and pin it for easy reuse.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example productivity workflows

- Daily planning
- Weekly review
- Meeting prep checklists
- Decision matrices
- Habit tracking prompts
