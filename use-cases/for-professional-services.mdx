Prompt Builder helps service teams create repeatable prompts for client work and deliverables.

## Common goals

- Standardize discovery and intake prompts
- Draft proposals faster
- Produce consistent client-ready summaries

## A simple workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build a baseline prompt">
    Use **Prompt Generator** to draft a structured prompt for a service task.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optimize for client delivery">
    Run it through **Prompt Optimizer** to refine clarity and format.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Store and reuse">
    Save the prompt in **My Prompts** for future engagements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example service workflows

- Discovery questionnaires
- Proposal drafts
- Client meeting summaries
- Scope and deliverable checklists
- ROI and impact summaries
