Prompt Builder helps founders create reliable prompts across product, marketing, and support.

## Common goals

- Draft onboarding and lifecycle messaging
- Standardize support replies and help center updates
- Create reusable product and marketing prompts

## A simple workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate a draft prompt">
    Use **Prompt Generator** to turn a goal into a structured prompt.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optimize for consistency">
    Run it through **Prompt Optimizer** to enforce format and constraints.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save as a template">
    Store the prompt in **My Prompts** for repeatable workflows.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example SaaS workflows

- Onboarding email sequences
- Feature announcement drafts
- Support response templates
- Product requirement outlines
- Customer feedback summaries
